ADAS Recalibration

Recalibration

Recalibrating ADAS systems after any windshield work isn't just about functionality; it's also about safety and compliance. Properly calibrated systems are essential for the vehicle to meet safety standards and operate as intended, ensuring your safety and that of others on the road.

At JFIPS, we understand the critical importance of Advanced Driver Assistance Systems (ADAS) in ensuring your safety on the road. That's why we offer precise and reliable ADAS recalibration services to ensure your vehicle's safety features function optimally after windshield replacements or repairs.

When it comes to ADAS recalibration after windshield services, trust JFIPS to deliver precision, reliability, and safety. Contact us today to schedule your recalibration service and keep your vehicle's safety features performing optimally.

Why Recalibration is Necessary:

  • Preserving Safety Features: ADAS systems rely on precise alignment and calibration to function correctly. Any change in the windshield, including replacements or repairs, can impact the sensors' positioning, potentially causing these safety features to malfunction.
  • Accurate Functionality: Recalibration ensures that cameras, sensors, and radar systems are correctly aligned and calibrated to provide accurate information to the vehicle's safety systems.
  • Preventing Errors: Failure to recalibrate after windshield replacement or repairs can lead to false warnings or complete malfunctions of safety systems, compromising the vehicle's safety.
